## Techniques:

LSV, CV (long-term stability test), EIS, Electrochemical workstation, etc. used to evaluate catalyst and electrode performance for related electrochemical systems.

<!--more-->

### Techniques
- Rotating disk electrode (RDE)
- Rotating ring-disk electrode (RRDE)
- Linear sweep voltammetry (LSV)
- Cyclic voltammetry (CV)
- Electrochemical workstation for Fuel cell and Battery testing
- Electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS)

### Focus areas
- ORR catalyst performance
- Current density and onset potential analysis
- Stability and degradation assessment
- Peroxide formation and reaction pathways
