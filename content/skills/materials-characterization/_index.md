## Materials Characterization

Experienced in structural, chemical, thermal, and morphological characterization of advanced materials for electrochemical and thermal energy systems.

<!--more-->

### Techniques and instruments
- Raman spectroscopy
- FTIR spectroscopy
- X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS)
- X-ray diffraction (XRD)
- Scanning electron microscopy (SEM)
- Transmission electron microscopy (TEM)
- Energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (EDS)
- Differential scanning calorimetry (DSC)
- Thermogravimetric analysis (TGA)

### Applications
- Graphene and nitrogen-doped graphene characterization
- MOF and composite materials analysis
- PCM thermal-property evaluation
- Structure–property relationship analysis
- Degradation and stability studies
